EX-001 CC-009 Service accounts without MFA Moderate Two service accounts identified without MFA enforcement during Q1 access review. Accounts had console access enabled. Management has disabled console access for both service accounts and implemented a detective control to alert on any future service account console logins. IT / IAM 2026-01-25 Remediated
EX-002 CC-010 Delayed access deprovisioning Moderate One terminated employee's access was deprovisioned 3 days after termination rather than on the last day of employment. Access during the gap was reviewed and no unauthorized activity was detected. Termination checklist updated to require same-day IAM deprovisioning sign-off. HR system now triggers automated deprovisioning ticket on last day of employment. IT / HR 2025-12-01 Remediated
EX-003 CC-012 Legacy firewall rule permitting broad inbound access Moderate A legacy firewall rule was identified permitting broad inbound access on port 443 from an unrestricted IP range. Rule dated from a prior project and was not removed during offboarding. Legacy rule removed. Firewall rule review process updated to include quarterly review of all inbound rules with aging rules flagged for justification or removal. Security Engineering 2026-01-20 Remediated
EX-004 CC-017 Emergency change missing rollback plan Minor One emergency change ticket reviewed lacked a documented rollback plan. Change was implemented successfully but rollback procedures were not documented prior to implementation. Change management policy updated to require rollback plan documentation even for emergency changes. CAB approval template updated to include mandatory rollback plan field. Engineering / Change Mgmt 2026-02-01 Remediated
EX-005 CC-018 Vendor reassessments overdue Moderate Two vendors were identified as overdue for annual security reassessment by 30 or more days. Both vendors have access to non-public data. Overdue assessments completed within 15 days of identification. Vendor risk calendar updated with automated reminders at 60 and 30 days prior to reassessment due date. Procurement / Security 2026-03-20 Remediated
EX-006 C-003 Overly broad role with access to confidential data Moderate One IAM role was identified with read access to a confidential data store broader than required for its function. Role was used by a reporting service. Role permissions scoped to specific data paths required for the reporting function. Principle of least privilege review scheduled for all data-access roles in Q2. IT / IAM 2026-02-10 Remediated
EX-007 C-005 Vendor missing current NDA Minor One third-party vendor was found to have an expired NDA. Vendor has ongoing access to non-public information under an active contract. Updated NDA executed with vendor within 5 business days. NDA renewal process added to annual vendor review calendar. Legal 2026-01-17 Remediated
